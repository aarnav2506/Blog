James Clear · Self-improvement

Atomic Habits

This book made discipline feel less dramatic and more practical. I like the idea that small systems, repeated daily, can quietly change the direction of your life.

Roald Dahl · Imagination

Roald Dahl

Dahl's stories feel mischievous, bold, and impossible to predict. They remind me that creativity works best when it is brave enough to be a little strange.

A classic adventure that still moves fast. The map, the sea, and the danger make it feel like a reminder that courage is built during uncertain journeys.
